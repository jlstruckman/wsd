Facts about Rats

  • Rats don't have thumbs.
  • Rats can eat chocolate.
  • A happy rat will chatter or grind its teeth.
  • Red discharge from a rat's nose or eyes is usually porphyrin, not blood.
  • Rats can be trained to do simple tricks.
