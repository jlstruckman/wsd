Welcome to Ratz Need Love!

This site is an information portal for people who either own rats as a pet or are interested in learning more about them.

Throughout the years, rats have gained quite the negative reputation. The goal of this site is to show people that rats are lovable, friendly creatures that deserve just as much respect as a standard house pet.

Facts about Rats

  • They can live up to 3 years.
  • Males are called bucks and females are called does.
  • They can fit through a hole as small as the size of their head.
  • You can give them baths!
  • They are very social so keep them in pairs or more.
